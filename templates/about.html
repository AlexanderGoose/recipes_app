About

This Recipe Management System is created by Alex Goose, designed to help users easily organize and explore recipes. Built on a robust foundation of modern web technologies, this application integrates Python, Flask, SQL, HTML, and CSS to provide a seamless and professional user experience.


Relying on Heroku for deployment and utilizing a postgres database, recipes are easy to access and safe from getting lost.


My mission is to only have to visit a food blog once. Gone are the days of relying on screenshots or dealing with annoying pop ups. Just add the recipe to the database and access it whenever you want.
